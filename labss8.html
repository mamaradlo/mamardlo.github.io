A dog is a domesticated mammal belonging to the Canidae family and the Canis lupus familiaris species. It is known for its close association with humans, serving as a companion and often kept as a pet. Dogs have been selectively bred over thousands of years for various purposes, such as hunting, herding, guarding, and assisting people with disabilities. They come in different breeds, each with distinct physical characteristics and temperaments. Dogs are known for their loyalty, intelligence, and social nature, making them popular and beloved animals worldwide.
